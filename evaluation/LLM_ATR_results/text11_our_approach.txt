The data subject shall have the right to receive the personal data concerning him or her, which he or she has provided to a controller, in a structured, commonly used and machine-readable format and have the right to transmit those data to another controller.
 The data subject shall have the right to have the personal data transmitted directly from one controller to another.
 The exercise of the right shall be without prejudice to and shall not apply to processing necessary for the performance of a task carried out in the public interest or in the exercise of official authority vested in the controller.
 The right shall not adversely affect the rights and freedoms of others.
